Objective

This data-driven strategy leverages a comprehensive, targeted advertising campaign to enhance Pharr International Bridge’s growth strategy and public reputation. All elements of the plan have been custom-created to achieve the following objectives:

  • Create brand and project awareness
  • Drive freight traffic to the Pharr International Bridge
  • Attract additional import and export traffic
  • Target specific audiences 
  • Build on the innovative and efficient reputation of the Pharr International Bridge
  • Establish authority in the industry and promote differentiating factors
Offer

We are to strategically position the Pharr International Bridge in order to appeal to the public and reach the campaign objectives. Having identified Unique Selling Propositions (USPs) of the project, we established that it will be most effective if we highlight rapid crossing times, superior logistics capabilities, and state of the art facilities utilizing F.A.S.T. and ACE.

Target Audience

We have identified the target demographic as decision-makers in the foreign trade, logistics, and customs fields of Mexican companies operating in the areas of Nuevo Leon, San Luis Potosi, Aguascalientes, and Queretaro, among others, who may not be aware of the specific and unique benefits afforded by utilizing Pharr International Bridge to move their shipments across the border.

To ensure they respond well to advertising messages, we will incorporate target audience data such as interests, behaviors, and job titles. We will also develop customized messaging geared towards resonating with these audiences. By targeting specific audiences most likely to be attracted to the Pharr International Bridge’s USPs, we are able to attract the maximum number of qualified customers.

Remarketing

What
Remarketing is a way to target your website's visitors with ads across the Internet. It helps you reach people who have previously visited your website, specifically people who have never converted into a lead or purchase. For example, if someone visits your "Contact Us" page but does not fill out the form, you can display ads to that person on the Internet, such as on Facebook, to get them to fill out the form.

Why
We use high-resolution banner ads targeted at users based on the extent of their activity on your website as well as the duration of time that has passed since their last engagement. Remarketing ads typically yield a lower cost-per-click along with a higher likelihood of resulting in a conversion. This is because remarketing campaigns focus specifically on a high-value audience that has already shown interest in your products. 

How
Remarketing uses an advertising network like Google Ads, Bing Ads, or Facebook. You create an advertisement to appear on the network of your choice, and the ad network places the ad on a variety of websites. When people on those websites visit your website, a cookie is dropped in their browser. The cookie allows your website to recognize the visitor, and then the ad network serves your ad to the person anywhere they go on the Internet.
